Pizza

Ingredients

  • Store Bought Dough
  • Warm Water
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese(Readers Choice)
  • Toppings(Readers Chocie)

Steps

  1. Stretch Dough (Dont Toss)
  2. Spead Tomato Sauce
  3. Sprinkle Cheese Evenly
  4. Drop On Toppings Evenly
  5. Bake at 350 Degress for 20-25 Minutes
  6. Cool Before Enjoying
