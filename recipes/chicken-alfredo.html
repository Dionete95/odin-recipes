Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Alfredo Pasta
  • Hot Water
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Broccoli
  • Milk

Steps To Make

  1. Cook Chicken Breast In Frying Pan
  2. Boil Alfredo Noodles
  3. Drain Water from Noodles
  4. Cut Chicken Breast Into Small Pieces
  5. Put Chicken Breast, Noodles, and Alfredo Sauce into New Pan
  6. Add Small Amount Of Milk
  7. Cook On Medium Until Simmering
  8. Add Broccoli To Mixture
  9. Stir Until Well Mixed
  10. Cool and Enjoy
